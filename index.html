Culture and History

Bihar is a land of profound history and diverse culture. The state is renowned for its contributions to education, religion, and the arts. The ancient Mauryan Empire flourished here, and iconic figures like Buddha and Mahavir were born in Bihar. The state's cuisine, festivals, and local crafts reflect its rich cultural heritage.
